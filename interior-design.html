INTERIOR DESIGN

CONSULTATION

Our interior design service specializes in creating sophisticated, immersive environments for luxury hotels. We blend timeless elegance with modern functionality to design spaces that elevate the guest experience—from grand lobbies and signature suites to fine dining areas, spas, and lounges. Every element is thoughtfully crated to reflect your brand’s identity, enhance comfort, and evoke a lasting impression. Whether launching a new property or reimagining an existing one, we deliver bespoke design solutions that embody style, refinement, and world-class hospitality. To optimize the design and furnishing, we recommend our professional onsite interior designer to advise you and give you the most important recommendations.

3-D DESIGN

Our 3D design service offers luxury hotels a powerful way to visualize and refine their spaces with precision and elegance. We create high-quality, photorealistic 3D models that bring every detail of your interiors to life—from grand lobbies and premium suites to restaurants, spas, and event spaces. This service allows you to explore design concepts, materials, furnishings, and lighting in a fully rendered environment before implementation. Perfect for renovations, new developments, or branding campaigns, our 3D designs help ensure every space reflects the luxury and sophistication your guests expect. This stage starts once we got all the information and preferences, such as budget, colours, fabrics and furniture style. Our designer will start doing conceptual design, schematic design and 3D.

3D ANIMATION

Our 3D animation service is designed to elevate the way luxury hotels present their spaces. We create immersive, cinematic walkthroughs that showcase your property’s elegance, atmosphere, and attention to detail—from grand lobbies and opulent suites to spas, dining areas, and outdoor spaces. These high-end animations help potential guests experience the ambiance and sophistication of your hotel before they arrive, making them a powerful tool for marketing, branding, and guest engagement. After the 3D design is confirmed and approved, 3D animation is provided if required. 3D animation offers the live view of your project.

FF&E SPCS

FF&E specifications refer to the detailed documentation outlining the furniture, fixtures, and equipment needed for a project. These documents are crucial for ensuring that all interior elements are accurately selected, described, and sourced to meet the design and functional requirements. They are essential for coordinating design, procurement, and seamless installation. Every piece of furniture and material used in the design is documented with dimensions, material, colour, pricing and source.

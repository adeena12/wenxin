FF&E SUPPLY AND APPLY

FURNITURE

We customize every piece of furniture to match your hotel interior design as we understand every hotel has its own branding signature design. Considering heavy use in the hotel, we use solid wood structure in most cases and fabrics used are fire retardant and Martindale Abrasion rating certified to ensure durability. Each piece of furniture is inspected to ensure it meets quality standards and specifications. Our product is packed with a non-woven cloth bag and EPE foam to avoid any possible scratch during transportation. Packed with foam and corrugated cardboard to protect the corner Protect the furniture from being damaged. Moreover, our price is very competitive along with high quality and aesthetically appealing which we are proud of.

CARPET & LINEN

In carpet production we use new Zealand wood or China wood, nylon, wool blends, and polypropylene for their stain resistance, durability, and ability to withstand heavy foot traffic specially in public areas. Most of our clients request Axminster carpet construction of 80% wool and 20%nylon. In linen production line we involves a multi-stage process, starting with the selection of raw materials like cotton or linen (flax), and then moving through yarn production, weaving, dyeing or bleaching, and finally, cutting, sewing, and finishing. Our quality standard for hotel linen is minimum 300TC for all beddings for star premium experience.

ACCESSORIES & ARTWORK

Artwork can play a signature role of decoration which makes a hotel complete. We have wide ranges of artwork frames for selection though solid wood frame is the most common one required by our clients. We use canvas or polyester with high quality ECO friendly ink to produce artwork by either printing ,semi-hand painting or complete hand painting with experienced artists. Printing and semi-hand painting artwork is waterproof which make it easy for dust cleaning. Every piece of artwork is well selected align with hotel interior design concept and culture. We use Butterfly screw for installation with security lock to ensure your hotel properties. Hotel accessories are displayed to enhance the interior design of the hotels.
